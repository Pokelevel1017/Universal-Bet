How To Play

The goal of Blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Number cards are worth their face value, face cards are worth 10, and Aces are worth 1 or 11. "Hit" to take another card, or "Stand" to keep your current hand. If your first two cards total 21, you have Blackjack (pays 2.5x)!
