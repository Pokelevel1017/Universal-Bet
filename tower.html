How To Play

Climb the tower to increase your multiplier! Choose one tile per floor. If you find a diamond, you move up; if you hit a bomb, the tower collapses and you lose your bet. You can cash out at any time after successfully clearing at least one floor. Higher difficulties have more bombs but offer massive rewards!
